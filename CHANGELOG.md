## 0.0.1

- **Initial Release:** A lightweight, highly scalable, and structurally complete Flutter Hooks framework alternatively styled around React-Hooks.
- **Core Engine:** Added `Hook`, `HookState`, `HookWidget`, and `HookElement` for uncompromised widget lifecycles.
- **Standard Hooks:** Shipped `useState`, `useEffect`, and `useMemoized`.
- **Controller Hooks:** Shipped `useScrollController`, `usePageController`, `useFocusNode`, and `useTextEditingController`.
- **Advanced Networking:** Shipped `useQuery`, `useMutation`, `useSubscription`, `useFuture`, and `useStream`.
- **Utility Hooks:** Shipped `useIsMounted`, `usePrevious`, `useDebounced`, `useValueListenable`, and `useListenable`.
- **Composed Domain Hooks:** Shipped `usePagination`, `useForm`, `useSearch`, and `useAnimationController`.
- **Verification:** Shipped complete test suite validations, documentation paths, and a unified Playground Example demonstrating all features interactively.
